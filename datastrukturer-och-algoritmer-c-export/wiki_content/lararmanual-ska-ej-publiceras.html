Workshops and group exercises

Workshops contains an shorter introduction by one of the teachers and then a set of exercises to be carried out in smaller groups. The exercises is more like tutorials and practical in C/Python. The aim is to focus on getting needed programming skills for the assignments later on. As a TA you get 1h in preparation and 2h for conducting the workshops.

Group exercises are more theoretical questions not connected to one special programming language. As a TA you get 2h in preparation and 2h for conducting the group exercises.

Tutoring

The tutoring on the courses will be done in Zoom and Tutorqueue. (Perhaps chat?). Avoid emails and refer questions not conerning assignments to the course responsible teachers.

You as TAs have the responsibility to add your tutor hours to the course calender in Canvas and to create a tutorqueue-meeting for it. See mail sent before the course start regardning when to place the tutoring hours and we can help you with the systems if needed.

During tutor hours your mission is to help students master the material as good as possible. Remember that giving straight answers is perhaps not the best way to create a learning situation. It might be better to ask them questions or refer them to the course material/course book/debugger. If you notice a lot of students having similar problems, it might be wise to create a special zoom-room for that question and refer students there.
